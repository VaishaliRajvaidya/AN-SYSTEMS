About GreenThumb Gardens

With over 15 years of experience serving clients across the UK, GreenThumb Gardens has established itself as a trusted name in professional gardening services. Our team of qualified horticulturists and landscape designers are passionate about creating and maintaining beautiful outdoor spaces.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, reliability, and commitment to sustainable gardening practices. Whether you need regular maintenance or a complete garden transformation, we work closely with you to achieve your vision while respecting your budget and timeline.

All our work is fully insured, and we offer free consultations and quotes. We serve residential and commercial clients throughout England, Scotland, and Wales.
